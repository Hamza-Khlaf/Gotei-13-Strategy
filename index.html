Executive Summary

This comprehensive digital marketing strategy is designed for a Gotei 13 specializing in online and local esports events for Valorant, Counter-Strike 2, Rocket League, FIFA, and League of Legends. The strategy focuses on building a strong presence across Facebook, TikTok, Instagram, LinkedIn, and Stories, targeting the growing Tunisian gaming community.

Key Market Facts

  • Tunisia's esports sector ranks 1st in Africa and 34th globally
  • Over 60% of Tunisia's population under 35
  • Mobile gaming dominates the market
  • Fertile ground for esports events and community building
